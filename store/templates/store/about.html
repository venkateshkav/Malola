How It Started

A Mom's Mission
Became a Movement

When Ovya couldn't find breakfast options her picky eater truly loved, she turned to her family's grain heritage — millets, ragi, oats, and quinoa passed down through generations. Together with her brother Pranav, she started experimenting in their home kitchen.

What began as weekend recipes soon became a brand. Malola — meaning "Super-Sibling" — was born to bring ancient, nutrient-dense grains to every modern family in formats kids actually want to eat.

Guided by our tagline "A Mom's Yes!", every product is designed to earn the trust of families by being safe, natural, and made with love.
