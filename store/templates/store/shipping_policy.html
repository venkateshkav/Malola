Shipping Policy

Home / Shipping Policy

At Malola Foods, we know how important it is for you to receive your organic and natural foods fresh, safe, and on time. That's why we take extra care to make sure every order—whether it's healthy snacks, cold-pressed oils, or millet-based products—is packed with love and delivered right to your doorstep with trusted delivery partners.

Where We Deliver

  • We ship across India—covering cities, towns, and even rural areas.
  • All products are dispatched directly from our facility in Salem, Tamil Nadu, so you enjoy truly farm-fresh food delivery.
  • Have a bulk or wholesale order? Just reach out to our support team, and we'll arrange the best custom shipping solution for you.

Processing & Delivery Timeline

  • Orders are usually processed within 24–48 hours after confirmation.
  • Delivery time is typically 3–7 business days, depending on your location.
  • Customers in metro cities may get their packages a little faster (3–4 days). For remote locations, it might take slightly longer.
  • Once your order is shipped, you'll receive tracking details via SMS/email so you can follow your package until it arrives.

Shipping Charges

  • Enjoy free shipping on prepaid orders above ₹999.
  • For orders below ₹999, a small shipping fee will be applied at checkout.
  • Cash on Delivery (COD) may be available in certain areas, with a minimal handling fee.

Packaging & Quality Promise

  • Every order is packed using eco-friendly, food-grade, and tamper-proof packaging to keep your food safe and fresh.
  • Before shipping, each item goes through a strict quality check.
  • Our commitment: No preservatives. No chemicals. Only 100% natural, organic, and healthy food—delivered with care.

International Shipping

At the moment, we deliver only within India. For overseas orders or partnerships, please write to us at malolafoods@gmail.com
